# Privacy Policy for Chit

**Effective Date: June 21, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application "Chit" (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). The App is designed to help users log their daily activities and sync them with their personal Google Calendar.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
**The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers owned by the developer.**
All activity records and settings created within the App are stored entirely locally on your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services (Google Calendar API)
The App uses the Google Calendar API to sync your activity logs to your personal Google Calendar.
* **Authentication:** The App uses Google Sign-In to authenticate your account securely.
* **Data Access:** The App only requests the minimum necessary permissions to create and manage specific calendars (e.g., "Chit") and add events to them.
* **Data Sharing:** Your calendar data is never shared with the developer or any other third parties. It is transmitted directly and securely between your device and Google's servers.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
chit.app.contact@gmail.com
